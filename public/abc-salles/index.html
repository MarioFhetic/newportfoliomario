ABC Salles

ABC Salles, leader in the rental of rooms for private events in France, recruited me to redo part of their website. Several missions were entrusted to me, the first one being to rethink the UI/UX design and then the development of the "request for quote" pages. The objective of UI/UX was to make a clean, modern design and to simplify the customer's journey as much as possible. Finally, my last mission was to create this time, a "magazine" part which would aim to create content, do inboud marketing to gain notoriety. For this part of the site I was able to create several features such as a DarkMode, a filtering system ...
